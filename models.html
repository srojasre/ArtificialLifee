LLamaDroid (Alpha)

This project implements a lightweight language model optimized for low-end Android devices, designed to serve as an accessible AI tutor for young learners in low-income households. Its goal is to enhance academic skills by providing personalized guidance, similar to a human tutor, ensuring educational support for all
